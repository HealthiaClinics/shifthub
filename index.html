Step 2 — EmailJS for notifications (free, optional)

1. Go to emailjs.com → sign up → Add Email Service (connect Gmail)
2. Create a template with these variables: {{employee_name}} {{clinic_name}} {{shift_date}} {{shift_time}} {{accept_link}}
3. Copy your Service ID, Template ID, and Public Key
